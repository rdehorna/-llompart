I got my B.A. in English Philology at the University of the Balearic Islands and I had the chance to spend my fourth year in Graz, Austria, on an ERASMUS study-abroad scholarship. This wonderful experience encouraged me to further my education far from home and I have not stopped since then. I have recently moved back to Europe in order to pursue a PhD in Phonetics and Speech Communication at the Ludwig-Maximilian University Munich. I love backpacking, all sports, India Pale Ales, Central European architecture and, now, Bavarian food too.
