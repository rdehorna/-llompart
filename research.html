Research

 

       I am a PhD student, under the supervision of Dr. Eva Reinisch, at the Institute of Phonetics and Speech Processing of the LMU Munich, where I also work as Research Associate on the project 'The impact of auditory feedback on error monitoring and phonetic category representation in a second language'. I am also a former member of the Arizona Applied Phonetics Laboratory at the University of Arizona. I am mostly interested in spoken word recognition, speech perception and experimental phonetics.
